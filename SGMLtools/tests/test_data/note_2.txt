<note><para>This procedure uses the MAN V/S CTL switch on the CABIN PRESS section of panel 25VU to control the cabin pressure. </para><para>This switch, spring loaded to neutral, controls the outflow valve position: </para><unlist bulltype="BULLET"><unlitem><para>UP: the outflow valve moves to the open position (the cabin pressure decreases) </para></unlitem><unlitem><para>DN: the outflow valve moves to the closed position (the cabin pressure increases). </para></unlitem></unlist><para>We recommend that: </para><unlist bulltype="BULLET"><unlitem><para>you operate this switch quickly (do not hold the switch in the UP or DN position) </para></unlitem><unlitem><para>you monitor the cabin vertical-speed indication to prevent sudden changes in cabin pressure. </para></unlitem></unlist></note>
